How To Make Favourite Chapati

Kenyan Layered Soft chapati

The Ingredients

  • 3 cups of flour
  • 1 table spoon salt
  • table spoon sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups of warm water

Steps of preparing the meal

  1. Add 3 cups of flour
  2. Add sugar and salt each 1 Table spoon
  3. Add oil to the mixture in the flour and sugar
  4. Kneadle the mixture for 20 Minutes
  5. After 40 minutes dough the flour in smaller balls
  6. Dust flour in a flat surface
  7. Dust flour in the flat place and take one of the balls and start rolling with a rolling pin to a circular shape
  8. Next, start rolling each of the coil-like shape doughs with the rolling pin to form a circular shape again.
  9. In a hot pan, place the rolled out circular chapati and fry each side with little oil until its golden brown on medium heat. Place your cooked chapati in a flat plat and cover with an aluminum foil
  10. Repeat this step to the rest of the coil-like dough

Now serve with your favourite stew
